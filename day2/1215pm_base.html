Base: 1215 PM - Head south

Head south—pass Harajuku’s edge. Enter via giant torii gates—see a forest of 100,000 trees hiding the city. Spot sake barrel stacks, a Shinto prayer wall.
Why It’s a Must-See: This Shinto shrine honors Emperor Meiji, who modernized Japan—it’s a spiritual escape where nature and tradition collide.
