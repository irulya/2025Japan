Long Story: 9 PM - Train back

The Hibiya Line hums, then the Yamanote clicks in—Tokyo’s nightlife flares outside: neon signs, salarymen stumbling from izakayas, wild fashion flashing by. The train’s a time capsule, carrying you from Roppongi’s gloss back to Ōkubo’s grit. The 300m walk home is quiet, Koreatown winding down. Crash by 9:30—day two’s done, and Tokyo’s under your skin.
