Long Story: 9 PM - 9PM

The Hibiya Line hums, then the Yamanote clicks in—Tokyo’s nightlife flares outside: neon signs, salarymen stumbling from izakayas, teens in wild fashion. The train’s a time capsule, carrying you from Roppongi’s gloss back to Ōkubo’s grit. The 300m walk home is quiet, Koreatown winding down. You crash by 9:30, legs aching, minds buzzing—day two’s done, and Tokyo’s already under your skin.
