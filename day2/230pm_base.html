Base: 230 PM - 230PM

Ride south—watch Tokyo shift. Hit the 52nd-floor observatory—see Shibuya’s sprawl, Skytree’s spike, maybe Fuji. Stroll the Mori Garden below: cherry blossoms, koi, a giant spider statue. Cafe break: coffee and pastries.
Why It’s a Must-See: The view’s a jaw-dropper—Tokyo’s endless pulse from 52 floors up. The garden’s quirky calm contrasts the urban rush.
