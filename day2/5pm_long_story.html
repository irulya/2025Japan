Long Story: 5 PM - Walk to Azabudai Hills

The walk to Azabudai Hills feels futuristic—glass towers gleam, Tokyo’s wealth on display. Then, teamLab Borderless hits: a universe of light. Tunnels pulse with color, mirrors multiply everything infinitely, and digital waterfalls crash without water. Glowing orbs drift; it’s art alive, wrapping you in chaos. Time slips away—a dream you don’t want to wake from. Japan’s cutting edge, unboxed.
