Base: 1015 AM - Stroll to this 144-acre oasis

Stroll to this 144-acre oasis—cherry blossoms explode in April. See pink petals over ponds, a tea house, and French gardens. Cameras click, calm settles in.
Why It’s a Must-See: Cherry blossoms at peak bloom are Japan’s soul in spring—Shinjuku Gyoen blends nature and history, a rare quiet spot in Tokyo’s chaos.
