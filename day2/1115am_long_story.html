Long Story: 1115 AM - Walk back through Ōkubo

The walk back through Ōkubo buzzes—Korean BBQ joints and trinket shops line the way, a mini Seoul in Tokyo. At Himawari Sushi, seats face the conveyor belt: tuna, veggie rolls, and eel roll past in a colorful stream. The chef’s a blur, slicing fish like a samurai. Plates stack up as the belt hums—it’s loud, lively, and your first real bite of Japan.
