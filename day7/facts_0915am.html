Nijo Castle's Nightingale Floors use metal clamps to chirp—tested in 1603, they foiled a 1634 plot. The castle housed 15 Tokugawa shoguns until 1868, when the last surrendered it to Emperor Meiji—ending 265 years of rule. Its 33 rooms span 3,300 square meters, dwarfing most Edo-era homes.
