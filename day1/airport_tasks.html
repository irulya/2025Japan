< ordered list1>Day 1 (03/31/25): Airport To-Dos at Haneda
  • Arrival at Haneda: Your Delta flight (DL0121) lands at Haneda Terminal 3 around 1 PM. Disembarking a full international flight with a family of six might take 15-20 minutes, especially if the grandparents move slower.
  • Passport Control: Haneda’s efficient, but lines can vary. For a midday arrival, expect 20-40 minutes. Japan’s immigration is straightforward—fill out arrival cards on the plane, and with no red flags, it’s quick. Six people might stretch it a bit, but you’re not peak-season slammed (Golden Week’s later).
  • Baggage Claim: With six+ suitcases, figure 20-30 minutes. Haneda’s baggage handling is fast, but locating and wrangling that much luggage takes time, especially with teens helping (or not).
  • Customs: If you’ve got nothing to declare (just personal stuff), it’s a 5-10 minute walk-through after a quick bag scan. If they pull you for questions—unlikely with a family—it could add 15 minutes.
  • Withdraw cash (yen) from ATM
  • Buy a local SIM card or rent a pocket WiFi
