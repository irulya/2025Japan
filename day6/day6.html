Day 6 (04/05/25): Hotel Vischio Kyoto to Tour Meet-Up

Starting your Kyoto tour today? Here’s how to get from Hotel Vischio Kyoto by GRANVIA (44-1 Kamitonodacho, Higashikujo, Minami-ku, Kyoto) to the meet-up spot near Kyoto Station’s Hachijo Exit at coordinates 34°58'59.7"N, 135°45'34.5"E—just 300-400 meters away. It’s a quick trip, so you’ll be there in no time!

Fastest Way: Walk

Distance: ~300-400 meters (~5-7 minutes)
How: Exit the hotel and head north toward Kyoto Station’s Hachijo Exit—you’ll see the station right across the street. Cross at the pedestrian signal, then walk east along the south side of the station. Look for tour signs near the Hachijo-guchi bus area, close to your coordinates.

Alternative: Taxi

Time: ~2 minutes
Cost: ¥600-¥800
How: Perfect if you’ve got bags or just want speed. Hop in a taxi outside the hotel, say “Kyoto Station Hachijo-guchi,” and point to 34°58'59.7"N, 135°45'34.5"E on your phone if needed—it’s a short ride to the tour spot.

Timing Tip

Leave 15-20 minutes early to pinpoint the exact meet-up (e.g., a tour bus bay). The area’s busy during Sakura season, so a little buffer helps.
