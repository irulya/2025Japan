Day 8 - Travel from Hotel Vischio Kyoto to Hotel Chinzanso Tokyo

Day 8 (04/07/25) - Travel from Hotel Vischio Kyoto by GRANVIA to Hotel Chinzanso Tokyo

Date: April 7, 2025 (Day 8)

Start: Hotel Vischio Kyoto by GRANVIA (44-1 Kamitonodacho, Higashikujo, Minami-ku, Kyoto, 34.98325°N, 135.75963°E)

End: Hotel Chinzanso Tokyo (2 Chome-10-8 Sekiguchi, Bunkyo City, Tokyo, 35.7116°N, 139.7271°E)

Departure: ~8:00 AM

Total Duration: ~3 hours (subway/walk) or ~2 hr 45 min (taxi option)

Total Cost: ~¥85,080 (subway) or ~¥88,680-¥89,180 (taxi)

Step-by-Step Itinerary

  • 7:45 AM - Depart Hotel Vischio Kyoto (15 min prep): Check out or grab bags—see Kyoto Station’s Hachijo Exit morning rush, commuters everywhere.
  • 8:00 AM - Walk to Kyoto Station (5-7 min, 300m): Head north—cross at Hachijo Exit signal, see the station’s sleek lines, maybe some cherry blossoms.
  • 8:07 AM - Board Shinkansen at Kyoto Station (2 hr 20 min ride, arrive 10:27 AM):
    • Train: JR Shinkansen Nozomi 6 (departs 8:07 AM, arrives Tokyo Station 10:27 AM)—reserved seats for six.
    • Platform: Central Concourse, tracks 11-12 (~5 min to board).
    • Cost: ¥14,030/adult, ¥7,015/child (14 & 16), total ¥84,180.
    • What to See: Kyoto’s edge fades, Osaka’s sprawl rolls by, Fuji might show if clear—teens dig the speed.
  • 10:27 AM - Arrive Tokyo Station, Choose Transfer Option (15-25 min): Exit Shinkansen at Marunouchi side—see Tokyo Station’s red-brick madness, busy crowds.
    • Option A: Subway to Korakuen + Walk (15-20 min):
      • Walk 300m (5 min) to Marunouchi Line subway (follow “Subway Marunouchi Line” signs).
      • Subway: Marunouchi Line to Korakuen (~10:35 AM-10:47 AM, 12 min, ¥150/adult, ¥75/child, ¥900 total).
      • Walk: Exit Korakuen (Exit 4a), 800m northwest (12 min)—see Bunkyo’s quieter streets, Chinzanso’s garden nearing.
      • Cost: ¥900 total.
      • What to See: Subway’s bright ads, residential shift—luggage manageable.
    • Option B: Taxi from Tokyo Station (15-20 min):
      • Walk to Marunouchi North Exit taxi stand (~5 min from Shinkansen gate).
      • Taxi: 4.5 km (15-20 min, ¥2,200-¥2,500/taxi, 2 taxis for 6 = ¥4,400-¥5,000 total)—light traffic at 10:30 AM.
      • Cost: ¥4,400-¥5,000 total.
      • What to See: Tokyo’s heart—Marunouchi towers, Kasuga-dori trees, hotel’s fancy entrance.
  • ~11:00 AM - Arrive Hotel Chinzanso Tokyo:
    • Subway/Walk: ~11:00 AM—drop bags (check-in ~3:00 PM), see the lobby’s polish, garden hints.
    • Taxi: ~10:45-10:50 AM—arrive faster, same drop-off scene.

Summary

Timeline:

  • Subway/Walk: 7:45 AM leave, 8:07 AM-10:27 AM Shinkansen, 10:27 AM-10:47 AM subway, 10:47 AM-11:00 AM walk, arrive ~11:00 AM.
  • Taxi: 7:45 AM leave, 8:07 AM-10:27 AM Shinkansen, 10:27 AM-10:45/50 AM taxi, arrive ~10:45-10:50 AM.

Cost:

  • Subway: ¥85,080 (¥84,180 Shinkansen + ¥900 subway).
  • Taxi: ¥88,680-¥89,180 (¥84,180 Shinkansen + ¥4,400-¥5,000 taxi).

Distance: ~550 km (Shinkansen), then ~1.1 km (subway + walk) or ~4.5 km (taxi).

Taxi’s an option—faster and simpler with luggage.
