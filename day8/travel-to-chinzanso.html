Day 8 (04/07/25): Travel from Hotel Vischio Kyoto to Hotel Chinzanso Tokyo

Move from Kyoto to Tokyo in ~2.75-3 hours, starting at Hotel Vischio Kyoto by GRANVIA (34.98325°N, 135.75963°E) and ending at Hotel Chinzanso Tokyo (35.7116°N, 139.7271°E). Total cost: ~¥85,080 (subway) or ¥88,680-¥89,180 (taxi).

Step-by-Step Itinerary

  • 7:45 AM - Depart Hotel Vischio Kyoto (15 min prep): Check out or grab bags—Kyoto Station’s Hachijo Exit hums with morning commuters.
  • 8:00 AM - Walk to Kyoto Station (5-7 min, 300m): Head north, cross at the Hachijo Exit signal—see the station’s sleek design, maybe cherry blossoms.
  • 8:07 AM - Shinkansen to Tokyo Station (2 hr 20 min): Board JR Shinkansen Nozomi 6 (departs 8:07 AM, arrives 10:27 AM)—reserved seats for six at ¥14,030/adult, ¥7,015/child (14 & 16), ¥84,180 total. Platforms 11-12, ~5 min to board. Watch Kyoto fade, Osaka pass, Fuji peek if clear—teens love the speed.
  • 10:27 AM - Transfer from Tokyo Station (15-25 min): Exit at Marunouchi side—Tokyo Station’s red-brick chaos, busy crowds.
    • Option A: Subway + Walk (15-20 min): Walk 300m (5 min) to Marunouchi Line, ride to Korakuen (10:35-10:47 AM, 12 min, ¥150/adult, ¥75/child, ¥900 total), walk 800m northwest (12 min)—Bunkyo’s quiet streets, Chinzanso’s garden nears.
    • Option B: Taxi (15-20 min): Marunouchi North Exit taxi stand (5 min walk), 4.5 km ride (¥2,200-¥2,500/taxi, ¥4,400-¥5,000 total for 2 taxis)—Marunouchi towers, Kasuga-dori trees, hotel’s grand entrance.
  • ~11:00 AM - Arrive Hotel Chinzanso Tokyo: Subway/Walk: ~11:00 AM; Taxi: ~10:45-10:50 AM. Drop bags (check-in ~3:00 PM)—see the lobby’s elegance, garden hints.

Summary

Timeline: Subway/Walk: 7:45 AM-11:00 AM (~3 hr); Taxi: 7:45 AM-10:45/50 AM (~2 hr 45 min).
Cost: Subway: ¥85,080 (¥84,180 Shinkansen + ¥900 subway); Taxi: ¥88,680-¥89,180 (¥84,180 Shinkansen + ¥4,400-¥5,000 taxi).
Distance: ~550 km (Shinkansen), then ~1.1 km (subway + walk) or ~4.5 km (taxi).
Tip: Taxi’s faster and simpler with luggage—worth it for six.
