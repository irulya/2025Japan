Day 10 - Getting to Haneda Airport

Day 10 (04/09/25): Getting to Haneda Airport

Options to reach Haneda Terminal 2 from Hotel Chinzanso Tokyo by 2:00 PM for your 4:20 PM flight—ranging from budget to luxe. Costs assume six people; adjust for fewer (~¥700 less per person missing).

Options

  • Public Transit (JR Yamanote + Tokyo Monorail): ~60-70 min, ¥4,200 total. Walk 1 km to Mejiro Station (15 min), JR Yamanote to Hamamatsucho (20 min, ¥200 each), transfer (5 min), Tokyo Monorail to Haneda Terminal 2 (20 min, ¥500 each). Cheap and reliable—trains every 2-10 min—but six suitcases make it a slog. Teens hauling bags through crowds? Rough.
  • Taxi: ~40-60 min, ¥16,000-20,000 total. Direct 20-25 km ride—¥8,000-10,000 per taxi, two needed (3 people + 3 bags each). Fastest in light traffic (~40 min), comfy, no transfers. Traffic could push 60 min—pricey but smooth. Hotel staff load bags, drop-off at departures.
  • Airport Limousine Bus: ~80-90 min, ¥8,800-9,300 total. Taxi to Ikebukuro Station (10 min, ¥1,000-1,500), bus to Haneda Terminal 2 (60-70 min, ¥1,300 each). Middle ground—comfy seats, luggage stored, but the Ikebukuro hop adds time. Buses every 30-60 min—check limousinebus.co.jp.
  • Private Limousine Service (Van): ~40-60 min, ¥20,000-25,000 total. Direct van (e.g., Toyota HiAce) fits 6 + six suitcases—booked via Tokyo MK or Klook. Luxe and seamless—driver handles bags, one vehicle keeps you together. Matches taxi speed, beats bus hassle.

My Recommendation

Top Pick: Private Limousine Van (~¥20,000-25,000)—six suitcases and 6 people fit perfectly, no splits, no stress. Leave 1:00 PM, arrive 1:40-2:00 PM—plenty for check-in. Book via hotel or Klook.
Runner-Up: Two Taxis (~¥16,000-20,000)—nearly as fast, split 3-3, no booking needed. Hail at 1:00 PM, arrive 1:40-2:00 PM.
Budget: Limousine Bus (~¥9,000)—cheaper, luggage-friendly, but 80-90 min with Ikebukuro detour hits 2:20 PM. Avoid public transit—six bags on trains is chaos.

Final Flow (Van Pick)

  • 9:30 AM - 10:15 AM: Breakfast at Chinzanso (~¥1,500).
  • 10:30 AM - 11:00 AM: Chinzanso Garden walk (~30 min).
  • 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM: Pack, check out—van arrives.
  • 1:00 PM: Leave—private van to Haneda (~40-60 min, ¥20,000-25,000).
  • 2:00 PM: Haneda Terminal 2—check-in, board by 4:20 PM.

Total: ~¥23,000-27,000 (breakfast, van)—zero walking with bags, luxe exit.
