Day 10 (04/09/25): Easy & Flexible, Depart Haneda 4:20 PM

A chill last day at Hotel Chinzanso Tokyo—minimal effort, quick local vibes, and a smooth exit. Leave by 1:00 PM via JR Yamanote (Mejiro to Hamamatsucho, 20 min, ¥200) and Tokyo Monorail (to Haneda Terminal 2, 20 min, ¥500). Arrive ~2:00 PM—2 hr 20 min before flight. ~¥700 one-way, ~¥2,200-2,700 total with breakfast.

Options

  • Shopping Near Hotel - Kagurazaka (30 min): 1.5 km (20-min walk) or 10-min taxi (¥1,000) from Chinzanso. Boutique shops on Kagurazaka-dori—tees, scarves, crafts (~¥1,000-3,000), La Kagu for basics. Quick 30-min stroll, grab one item—light shopping, no rush. See quaint streets, maybe late blossoms.
  • Short Walk in Park - Chinzanso Garden (30 min): Right at the hotel—free, no travel. Pagoda, ponds, late Sakura vibes—short 30-min loop, sit by the pond. Easiest wind-down, calm and compact.
  • Interesting Streets - Mejiro-dori Stroll (30 min): 500m (10-min walk one-way) toward Mejiro Station. Quiet street—stationery, pastries, casual tees (~¥500-2,000). 30-min out-and-back, peek in a shop—simple last look at local life.

Flow (Chinzanso Garden Pick)

  • 9:30 AM - 10:15 AM: Breakfast at Chinzanso (~¥1,500)—fuel up in style.
  • 10:30 AM - 11:00 AM: Garden walk (~30 min)—relax, snap pics.
  • 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM: Pack, check out—2 hr buffer.
  • 1:00 PM: Leave for Haneda—JR to Hamamatsucho, Monorail (~45-60 min, ¥700).
  • 2:00 PM: Arrive Haneda Terminal 2—check-in, board by 4:20 PM.

My Take

Top Pick: Chinzanso Garden—zero hassle, right there, max packing time after a 30-min stroll. Perfect breather post-Day 8’s sumo and full day—teens might not rave, but it’s smoothest.
Kagurazaka: Quick shopping—20-min walk’s tight, ¥1,000 taxi keeps it viable. Quality finds, less time to browse.
Mejiro-dori: Middle ground—light shopping, short walk, less charm than Kagurazaka or garden calm.
