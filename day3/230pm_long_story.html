Long Story: 230 PM - Stroll southwest

The walk southwest shifts from Asakusa’s old charm to Akihabara’s glow—retro signs give way to anime billboards. Shops like Animate stack manga, arcades blare, maid cafes beckon with frilly calls. It’s otaku central, born from post-war radio markets into a ¥150 billion hub. Teens won’t miss this—crammed with games and gear, it’s Tokyo’s wild nerd soul.
