Long Story: 12 PM - Head south

The ride south brushes Asakusa’s retro vibe, landing you at Tsukiji’s outer market. Stalls line tight alleys—sushi bites glisten, tamagoyaki sizzles, fish skewers smoke on grills. It’s the gritty half of Tsukiji’s legacy, thriving after the 2018 auction shift to Toyosu. Vendors shout, knives flash; it’s a chaotic feast, pure and unfiltered. Dig in—Tokyo’s sea is on your plate.
