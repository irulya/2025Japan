Long Story: 5 PM - Walk south

The walk south winds past trendy spots, landing you at Shibuya Crossing. It’s a flood—3,000 cross when the light turns, neon ads blaze overhead, chaos moves like clockwork. Born from post-war bustle, it’s now a global icon—raw, loud, and alive. Phones catch the madness; this is Tokyo’s beating heart.
