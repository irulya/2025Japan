Base: 10 AM - Train east

Train east—pass Sumida River. At 11:00 AM, hit the 350m deck—see Tokyo’s endless rooftops, maybe Fuji. Glass walls frame the city’s scale.
Why It’s a Must-See: Skytree’s the world’s tallest tower—634m of vertigo and views, a soul-stirring peek at Tokyo’s sprawl.
