Long Story: 4 PM - Train west

The train rolls west, Tokyo morphing into Harajuku’s vibe. Takeshita Street hits—400m of kawaii overload: crepe stands waft strawberry, purikura booths snap glittery pics, lolita fashion struts in frills. It’s a teen dream since the ‘70s, a chaotic bubble of shops and screams. Teens won’t miss this—it’s Tokyo’s loudest, brightest soul.
