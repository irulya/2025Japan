Long Story: 10 AM - Train east

The train cuts east, Sumida River glinting below. You arrive by 11:00 AM at Skytree—its 350m deck is a glass-walled perch over Tokyo’s chaos: rooftops roll out, Skytree’s shadow stabs the city, and Fuji might peek if clouds part. It’s 634m of steel and awe, opened 2012—a modern marvel dwarfing the past. The scale sinks in; this is Tokyo unbound.
